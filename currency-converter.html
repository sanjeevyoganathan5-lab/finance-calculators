GBP to USD and Other Currency Conversions

The GBP to USD exchange rate fluctuates constantly based on economic data, interest rate decisions from the Bank of England and Federal Reserve, geopolitical events, and market sentiment. As of 2025, the pound typically trades between 1.20 and 1.30 against the dollar, though this varies significantly throughout the year.

When converting currency for travel, international payments, or business purposes, be aware that banks and money transfer services charge a spread on top of the mid-market rate shown here. Specialist providers like Wise or Revolut typically offer rates much closer to the mid-market rate than high-street banks.
