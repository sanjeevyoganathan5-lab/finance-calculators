How Long Does It Take to Pay Off a Credit Card?

Credit card debt is expensive because of compound interest — your card charges interest on the balance each month, which gets added back onto what you owe. At a typical UK APR of 24.9%, a £3,500 balance paid off at £150 per month takes around 28 months and costs over £650 in interest alone.

The minimum payment trap is one of the most costly mistakes — credit card minimum payments are often just 1–2% of the balance, meaning it can take decades to clear a balance and you'll pay several times the original amount in interest. Even paying £20–£50 more per month can save hundreds in interest and cut years off the repayment time.
